# Parts & Suppliers

## BioThane Webbing (Collar Strap)

**Paracord.eu** — best EU option, ships to Lithuania.
- Buy per meter or in 30m rolls
- BioThane BETA in widths: 13mm, 19mm, 25mm, 38mm
- Price: ~€1.50–2.50/meter
- 1 collar uses ~35–60cm of strap; a 30m roll yields approximately 50–80 collars
- URL: paracord.eu

**For bulk purchasing:**
- biothane.us/international-distributors — lists EU distributors
- Notable EU distributors: Worring Leder (DE), Abbey (UK)

## Silicone Letter Charms

**Alibaba** — primary source for small and medium quantities.
- MOQ as low as 10–20 pieces at many suppliers
- Search terms: `"silicone letter charms dog collar wholesale"`, `"TPE pet collar slide charms personalized"`, `"silicone alphabet charms pet tag"`

**Recommended supplier:**
- **Dongguan Altra Pet Products Co., Ltd.** — altrapet.en.alibaba.com — specialized pet accessories factory

**Pricing:**
| Quantity | Cost per charm |
|----------|---------------|
| Small MOQ (10–50) | ~€0.20–0.50 |
| 500+ units | ~€0.08–0.15 |
| 5-letter set (small MOQ) | ~€1.00–2.00 |

**IMPORTANT — EU Compliance:** Always request a **REACH compliance certificate** for silicone from any Alibaba supplier. This is required for legal sale within the EU.

**IMPORTANT — Charm Mounting Detail:** Charms have holes on the **LEFT and RIGHT sides only**. The collar strap threads horizontally through each charm from side to side — charms sit flat and flush on top of the strap. They do NOT hang as pendants and are NOT threaded top-to-bottom. Verify this construction with any new supplier before placing an order.

## Assembly

BioThane requires a **walking-foot sewing machine** — a standard sewing machine will not work well on this material.

**Alternatives:**
- Find a local tailor or leatherworker in Vilnius or Kaunas
- Search Facebook groups: `"siuvėja šunų apykaklė"` or similar
- Leatherwork studios may also be willing to take small collar runs
