# Brand Identity

## Brand Name

**Pawlette** — recommended primary name.

- English/international; communicates color palette, customization, and dogs in a single word
- Previous Lithuanian name considered: **Žavesys** (means "enchantment" or "charm" in Lithuanian) — still viable as a local/secondary brand identity or handle
- Target handles: `@pawlette` or `@zavesys`

## Vibe & Positioning

"Pangaia for dogs" — premium-feeling but approachable. Pastel, playful, and personality-driven. The brand celebrates each dog's individual character through color and name personalization.

- Tone: friendly-casual
- Feel: boutique, handcrafted, gift-worthy
- Audience: dog owners aged 20–35 who treat their pet as a family member and care about aesthetics

## Color Palette

| Role | Name | Hex |
|------|------|-----|
| Primary / CTA | Sage Meadow | `#A8D5A2` |
| Background | Cream | `#FAF7F2` (never pure white) |
| Accent 1 | Blossom Pink | (pastel pink) |
| Accent 2 | Sky Dew | (powder blue) |
| Accent 3 | Honey Drop | (butter yellow) |
| Text / Dark | Bark | `#3D3530` |

These map directly to the CSS custom properties already in the codebase (`--color-sage`, `--color-cream`, `--color-blossom`, `--color-sky`, `--color-honey`, `--color-bark`).

## Typography

**Display / Headings:** Fredoka One (Google Fonts, free)
- Chunky, bubble-rounded — closest free match to the charm lettering style
- Alternatives: Baloo 2 ExtraBold, Nunito Black

**Body / UI:** DM Sans

**Handwritten accent:** Caveat

> Note: The current codebase uses Luckiest Guy for headings. Fredoka One is the brand-forward recommendation for future visual identity work and marketing materials. Do not swap fonts in code without a deliberate migration.

## Platforms

- **Instagram** — primary discovery channel
- **Website** — product configurator + conversion

## Instagram Content Strategy

Three content pillars:

1. **Product Hero** — clean flat-lay shots of collars and charms against cream backgrounds. Showcase color combinations, new name spellings, limited-edition charm drops.
2. **Dog Personality** — user-generated or styled content featuring real dogs wearing Pawlette collars. Emotional, shareable, builds community.
3. **Education** — care tips (how to clean BioThane, how to measure neck), styling guides, "which collar for which dog breed" type posts. Drives SEO traffic and positions brand as expert.

Post frequency target: 4–5x per week (mix of feed + stories + reels).
