# Competitive Landscape

**Market focus: Lithuania + Baltic region**

---

## Key Competitors

### Springland Pets
- **Threat level: Medium**
- Sells silicone charm collars with international reach
- Already established with brand recognition in the customizable collar segment
- Main risk: they have a head start on awareness and trust
- Strategic response: compete on local identity, faster delivery, and a more personal brand story

### Distinguish Me (Kaunas)
- **Threat level: HIGH — most dangerous local competitor**
- Handmade collars, BioThane waterproof construction, local Lithuanian brand
- Has built local trust and is already in the same material category
- Currently does NOT offer a customizable letter charm system — this is the gap
- If they add a charm system before Pawlette launches, a core differentiator disappears
- **Action: move fast. First-mover advantage matters here.**

### Bakli.lt
- **Threat level: Low-Medium**
- Local Lithuanian pet collar seller
- Competes more on price than on premium positioning
- Less of a direct threat to a boutique charm-collar brand

### Wear Felicity
- **Threat level: Medium**
- Waterproof silicone dog collars with charm system
- International brand, not Lithuania-specific
- Similar product category but lacks the BioThane + local angle

### LT Marketplaces (Pigu.lt, Varle.lt, etc.)
- **Threat level: Low (on brand terms)**
- Generic commodity competition on price
- Not relevant if Pawlette competes on brand, customization, and aesthetics rather than price

---

## Key Strategic Insight

**No Lithuanian brand currently combines:**
- BioThane waterproof construction
- Customizable letter charms (personalized names)
- Pastel brand identity and boutique packaging

That combination is the market gap. The opportunity is real and the window is open — but Distinguish Me is the one competitor capable of closing it quickly.

**Priority:** Get a first batch photographed and selling before Distinguish Me pivots toward personalization. Even a small Instagram presence with product photos establishes the brand.
